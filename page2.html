Largest employers

[edit]

Below is the list of largest employers including government owned companies and institutions in 2022.

Rank Employer Employees (mn) Country State-owned Ref
1 Ministry of Defence (India) 2.99  India [1]
2 Department of Defense (USA) 2.93  United States [1]
3 People's Liberation Army 2.55  China [1]
4 Walmart 2.30  United States [1]
5 Amazon 1.61  United States [1]
6 China National Petroleum 1.45  China [1]
7 National Health Service 1.38  United Kingdom [1]
8 Foxconn 1.29  Taiwan [1]
9 Indian Railways 1.21  India [2]
10 Tata Group 1.02  India [3]
